OUR MISSION TO CLARITY

THE QUELL FOUNDATION WORKS TO EDUCATE COMMUNITIES ABOUT ISSUES AND CONCERNS SURROUNDING MENTAL HEALTH, PROMOTING OPEN, JUDGEMENT-FREE DIALOGUE

TRAINING

Donations to The Training Fund prepare first responders to recognize & respond to signs of mental distress within their ranks, teach law enforcement agencies advanced mental health crisis intervention skills, and supports service dog training throughout the country.

ACCESS

Donations to Project Access will increase the talent pipeline through scholarship awards to students entering the mental healthcare profession, expand delivery of needed mental health care services, and promote reformation to extend the scope of clinical behavioral health professionals services.

Awareness

Donations to The Awareness Fund will educate communities and individuals in care disparities. The Funds aim is to reduce the number of suicides, drug overdoses, and incarceration of people with a treatable mental illness.

SCHOLARSHIPS

WE ARE OFFERING FOUR SCHOLARSHIPS TO DEMONSTRATE OUR COMMITMENT TO, AND APPRECIATION FOR, ADVANCING EDUCATION FOR THOSE BATTLING MENTAL HEALTH CONDITIONS, AND/OR DEDICATED TO A CAREER IN THE MENTAL HEALTH PROFESSION
